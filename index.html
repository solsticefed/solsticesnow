About

My name is Sol, I'm from Airlie in Angus and snowboarding was a sport that made a big impression on me when I was young. After finishing my studies, I decided to start chasing snowboarding. The past few years I've been instructing in Austria (Lech am Arlberg) but I am now looking to be in Scotland permantely, and so I will try share my passion for snowboarding with the people in the beautiful country of Scotland!

My snowboard instructing journey started in New Zealand, then to France, and finally to Austria. In 2022 I completed my Anwärter (lvl 1+2), in 2023 I started and finished my Landeslehrer-II (lvl 3) which included the Alpine guiding course led by Snowsports Academy.

You will need basic snowboarding gear, including a snowboard, boots, and a helmet. I do not offer rental equipment.
Please get in touch if you have a custom inquiry regarding prices/length/type of booking. A group day consists of 4 hours of lesson time, with a recommended break of 1 hour half way through.
